I'm a second year **Artificial Intelligence** student at **Radboud University** (Nijmegen, NL) with a non linear path that shaped who I am as a builder and problem solver.

My relationship with computers started at age 6. By 10, I was deep in Minecraft command blocks my first real exposure to algorithmic thinking before I had a name for it. That curiosity has never left.

I managed operations, hygiene compliance, prep, closing procedures, and documentation, often as the only person on shift. These weren't gap years they were a different kind of education: logistics, ownership, and performing under pressure.

I applied to Radboud's AI programme during that stretch. Got accepted. Started September 2025.

I hold **dual EU citizenship** (TR/BG) and am actively looking for **internship / working-student / entry-level opportunities** in AI, ML, or related engineering roles  open to **Netherlands, Germany, Switzerland, Ireland**, and **remote**.
